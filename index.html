DIGITAL SINGLE
DYNAMITE

BTS's digital single released on August 21, 2020 and track 8 of the album "BE" released on November 20, 2020. The new song Dynamite is a retro pop genre song that was completed with the intention of revitalizing the time when it was suffering from Covid-19.

BILLION VIEW

HITS

It is the first K-POP song to top the Billboard charts at home and abroad. It was nominated for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance at the 63rd Grammy Awards.

ABOUT BTS

BTS is a seven-member South Korean boy band debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. It is very popular both at home and abroad and is regarded as the representative idol group of the current generation of K-POP.
